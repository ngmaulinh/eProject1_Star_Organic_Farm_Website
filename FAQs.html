Orders & Payment

To bring you the best online shopping experience, we offer multiple payment methods:
  1. Cash On Delivery(COD)
  2. Credit/Debit Card
  3. ATM Card
  4. eM Wallet
  5. MoMo Wallet
Star Organic Farm accept all Visa and Master Cards, both Credit and Debit, and is 3D Secure(Verified by Visa, and MasterCard Secure) enabled. All your credit card information are protected by means of industry-leading encryption standards.
Star Organic Farm does not collect any fee when you make by credit card on website. We just receive the right amount printed on the invoice or sales recept.
However,in some cases that your card is not issued in Vietnam, you properly have to pay the fee for foreign currency exchange to the card issuing bank. If you are not sure of this fee, kindly check with your card issuing bank for the more information.
COD stands for Cash On Delivery. With this payment method, you can pay in cash to the delivery agent upon receipt of your order. We accept COD nationwide.
Simple. Secure. Hassle-fee! Enjoy having a peace of mind when you purchase using COD at Star Organic Farm.
There are may reasons why the payment mode, COD, may not be available to you as a payment method.
  1. Sellers did not select 3PLs that provides COD as an option
  2. Carting out of the multiple orders with various shopping options
  3. COD is removed as a payment method
  4. Seller is out of COD coverage area
Once your order is confirmed, you will receive an order confirmation email. You will find information on how to track your order on our website.


Shipping & Delivery

After you have successful placed your order, our Customer Service team will conduct a verification process and update you as soon as possible by email.
We offer 2 shipping options: Standard and Express Shipping.
  1. Standard Shipping : Delivery time depends on your location and location of the products, i.e. Star Organic Farm warehouse or merchant warehouse
  2. Express Shipping: Order before 17:00 for same day delivery, order after 17:00 for next day delivery(excluding Sundays and Public holidays)
Checking orders on Star Organic Farm is easier and more convenient with 3 following ways:
  1. Via Star Organic Farm app
  2. At order tracking tool of Star Organic Farm
  3. Via Website
  1. Shipping fee is also shown at product page after you input shipping address
  2. Shipping fee is also shown on your shopping cart at check-out page


Returns & Refunds

In case the purchased products are defective, not genuine or not match with the product's description on Website, we offer different return policies depending on different categories.
  1. Check Retuns & Refunds Policy: Before returning an item, please check out our Returns & Refunds Policy to make sure your item is eligible for return
  2. Choose the order you want to return
  3. Fill up the refund form
  4. Choose shipment method and refund method
  5. Print/wirte down return label
  6. Pack the item and send it to Star Organic Farm
  7. Wait result
After we have received your item at our warehouse, we will process the quality evaluation then send you an email and SMS notification within 2-3 working days.
Your refund will be process after we get the quality check result and we will inform you via email/SMS. For your convenience, we offer different refund options based on your payment method of returned order.
